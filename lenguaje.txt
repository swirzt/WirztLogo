Comm ::= Comm Comm 
       | forward Exp | fd Exp
       | back Exp | bk Exp
       | right Exp | rt Exp
       | left Exp | lt Exp
       | clearscreen | cs
       | clean
       | penup | pu
       | pendown | pd
       | hideturtle | ht
       | showturtle | st
       | home
       | setx Exp | sety Exp | setxy Exp Exp
       | setheading Exp | seth Exp
       | repeat Exp [Comm]
       | print Exp
       | print String
       | setcolor Exp
       | for [str Exp Exp Exp] [Comm]
       | for [str Exp Exp] [Comm]
       | if Exp [Comm] [Comm]
       | to String Args Comm end
       | make String Exp
       | wait Exp
       | do.while [Comm] Exp
       | while Exp [Comm]
       | Strin [Exp]
       | scale Exp
       | arc Exp Exp
       | label Str
       | setlabelheight Exp
       | skip
       
Exp ::= Float
        | - Exp
        | xcor | ycor
        | heading
        | towards Exp Exp
        | :var
        | Exp + Exp
        | Exp - Exp
        | Exp * Exp
        | Exp / Exp
        | readword -- Lee una Exp de consola
        | if Exp Exp Exp
        | Exp > Exp
        | Exp < Exp
        | Exp = Exp
        | Exp <= Exp
        | Exp >= Exp
        | Exp != Exp
        | Exp && Exp
        | Exp || Exp
        | not Exp
        | true
        | false
        | random Exp -- NÃºmero random entre 0 y Exp
        | ( Exp )
